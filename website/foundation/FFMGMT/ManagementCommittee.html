The Management Committee is responsible for the day to day running of the FirebirdSQL Foundation. They are elected annually at the annual general meeting.

Decisions and minutes of meetings:

The Committee holds its meetings in a newsgroup and makes decisons according to an online voting procedure.

The newly-formed Management Committee is testing software developed "in-house" which automatically manages and posts to pages in this site:
  • Questions under current consideration
  • Questions currently being voted on
  • Questions previously decided
