Report from the committee meeting
The committee held its January meeting in the ffmembers forum, to give the members an impression of what the committee does and what it involves being on the committee.

Surprisingly, even though there were ten times as many attendees as in a normal committee meeting, the number of emails was lower than usual. General discussions concerned the roles of incoming new committee members, some special resolutions to be presented at the Annual General Meeting and further discussion of plans regarding Mozilla's use of our Firebird brandname for their browser.
