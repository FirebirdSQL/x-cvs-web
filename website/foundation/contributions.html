Contributing to Firebird


There are many ways you can contribute to the developement of the FirebirdSQL project. You can:
  • consider becoming a member of the FirebirdSQL Foundation.
  • make a donation, or
  • become a sponsor of the Firebird project.
All monies raised are spend at the discression of the FirebirdSQL Foundation
