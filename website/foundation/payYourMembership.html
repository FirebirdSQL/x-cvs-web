Voting Membership

 Voting memberships form the "backbone" of the funding pool for our Firebird development projects. As a voting member you can play a real part in providing the ongoing support that will keep regular coordination, maintenance and development of wish list items going. You can nominate officers at Foundation elections and be nominated for office, propose and vote on issues of general concern and join committees, including those which decide the outcome of grant recommendations and other financial matters.
  • One-year subscription $300 USD (recurring automatically)
  • One-year subscription $300 USD (single payment)
  • Monthly subscription $25 USD per Month (recurring automatically)


Associate Membership

As an Associate member, you can make yourself part of the movement to provide a funding basis for Firebird development, without the degree of financial commitment that Voting membership requires. You will become part of the general forum for Foundation meetings and can freely contribute your views and suggestions. You will be eligible to join committees for any purpose other than those concerned with the disbursement of grants and other financial matters.
  • One-year subscription $50 USD (recurring automatically)
  • One-year subscription $50 USD (single payment)
