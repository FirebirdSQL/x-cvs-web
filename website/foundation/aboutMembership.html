Becoming a Member


If you are interested in joining the FirebirdSQL Foundation, please:
  1. Read the rest of this page and browse the rules of the association to ensure you understand the costs involved and your obligations as a member.
  2. Fill in and post the application form here
Your application will be looked at as soon as possible by the committee.

Eligibility to apply for membership is open to all individuals and organisations who accept and promote the objects and rules of the Association. The Committee shall in its sole discretion determine whether or not to accept an application for membership.

Foundation membership is available in two types:

Voting Membership

 A Principal or Voting Member has normal voting rights at general meetings. This gives you a say in determing who are the elected officers of the Foundation.
The current cost and payment options for Voting membership are:
 
  • 1 Year subscription $300us

  • 1 Year subscription paid monthly at $25us/Month


Associate Membership

An Associate or Non-voting Member is identical to a Voting member, except thay do not have voting rights at general meetings. This gives you a say in determing who are the elected officers of the Foundation.
The current costs and payment methods for Associate membership are:
 
  • 1 Year $50us

Full details on membership and the operation of the Foundation can be found in the Rules of the Association
