Firefox - A New Brand of Browser

Monday 9 February 2004
Today there's a new brand of web browser in the market: it is open source and its name is Firefox.

To be precise, it is v.0.8 of the new "light" web browser the Mozilla crew has been developing to replace the bloated Mozilla browser that evolved from the original Netscape Navigator.

For many months, the Mozilla crew has been using the project's code-name for the browser--Firebird--to promote it widely in many public forums and news sites. The problems this caused for the Firebird Project are well-known.

So it's with a great sense of relief that we welcome Mozilla's firy fox into the barnyard and congratulate them for finding such a superb creature to blaze their trail.

Well done, Mozilla team!
 
from the Firebird team
