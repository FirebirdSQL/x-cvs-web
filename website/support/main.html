Reporting Bugs

Before submitting a bug, please check the the Firebird FAQ.

If the FAQ doesn't answer your question, report a bug to our bug tracker.

When writing a bug report, please try to be as specific as possible. You should specify your Firebird version, architecture, operating system, any relevant output from the activity log, and an e-mail address, in case we need further information from you to fix the bug.
